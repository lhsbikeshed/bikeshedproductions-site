Production History

Our history so far.

  • Feb - 2013

    Hang on, I've got a plan

    Mid Febuary Charles bought an old caravan to kickstart our project. We had 2.5 months to strip it down and build it out as a full threatrical experience ready for makerfaire in Newcastle.

  • April 2013

    Just in time

    With the final outfitting finished intime and the programming installed, we transported the caravan up from London to Newcastle ready for its debut.

  • Time Passes

    Bigger and better

    We continued to build out the experience, swapping out whole sections of set, new story lines and tech. Touring it around the country to many varied events we fine tuned the experience.
